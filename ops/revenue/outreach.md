# Outreach

Short founder-led scripts for starting conversations about operational muddle and savings-share work.

## Cold Email

Subject: reducing avoidable cost at [Company]

Hi [Name],

I work with founder-led teams to find operational muddle, remove wasted work, and measure the savings.

If there is a process at [Company] that feels slower, noisier, or more expensive than it should, I can usually diagnose it quickly and propose a simple savings-share arrangement: if we prove savings, we share in them.

Open to a short call next week?

Best,
[Your name]

## LinkedIn DM

Hi [Name] - I'm reaching out because [Company] looks like the kind of operation where small workflow leaks can add up quickly.

I help teams identify operational muddle, clean it up, and capture verified cost savings. If useful, I can share a very short diagnostic call outline.

## Referral Intro

I'm building Proactiva around a simple offer: we find the muddle, fix part of it, verify the savings, and take a share of what we prove.

If you know a team that is clearly losing time or money to process confusion, I'd like an introduction.

## Voicemail

Hi [Name], this is [Your name]. I help teams find and remove operational muddle and tie my fee to verified savings. I'll send a short note as well. My number is [number].

## Follow-Up

Quick follow-up, [Name].

The offer is still simple: diagnose the bottleneck, implement a cleaner workflow, and charge against verified savings.

If there is someone on your side who owns operations, finance, or process improvement, I'd be glad to speak with them.
