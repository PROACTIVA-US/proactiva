# Proactiva Revenue Operating Manual

Last updated: 2026-04-14

## Revenue Thesis

Proactiva makes money by resolving operational muddle.

The offer:

1. find a costly workflow problem,
2. define the current cost,
3. implement a focused fix,
4. verify the savings,
5. charge a percentage of verified savings.

## Current Narrow Market

Start with property management because it often has:

- high-volume tenant and owner communication,
- visible response-time complaints,
- repeated handoffs,
- maintenance coordination problems,
- clear operational costs from delay and rework.

This is a starting hypothesis, not a permanent niche.

## Daily Loop

1. Read `projects/proactiva/revenue/next-actions.md`.
2. Complete the `Next Operator Action`.
3. Let Vine Master complete the `Next Autonomous Action`.
4. Check the visible Vine Master heartbeat message.
5. Confirm the next action did not fabricate leads or outreach.
6. Move one step closer to a discovery call.

## Lead Qualification Rules

A lead can enter `lead-list.md` only if there is a real public source or direct referral.

Required fields:

- organization name,
- category,
- why it might fit,
- verified contact source,
- current status,
- next step.

Valid pain signals:

- repeated public complaints about response time,
- hiring posts for operations roles that suggest overload,
- visible process complexity,
- public reviews mentioning maintenance, billing, intake, scheduling, or follow-up problems,
- referral saying the business has operational friction.

Invalid lead data:

- guessed contacts,
- invented emails,
- fake outreach status,
- assumed savings without a measurable workflow.

## Outreach Sequence

1. Identify one specific company.
2. Find one concrete pain signal.
3. Identify a decision-maker or public contact path.
4. Draft one short message using `outreach.md`.
5. Mark outreach as drafted, not sent.
6. Only mark outreach as sent after the operator actually sends it.

## Discovery Call Goal

The call must answer:

- what process is stuck,
- who owns it,
- what it costs now,
- what would count as savings,
- what data can verify savings,
- who can approve a pilot.

Use `discovery-call.md` as the guide.

## Savings-Share Proposal Shape

A proposal should name:

- client,
- workflow,
- baseline,
- measurement period,
- excluded unrelated changes,
- Proactiva share of verified savings,
- optional setup fee,
- payment timing.

Use `savings-share-template.md` as the starting point. Final terms need legal review.

## Replication Pack Rule

After one workflow fix works once, turn it into a replication pack immediately.

Do not wait for perfection. A pack needs:

- problem definition,
- signs the problem exists,
- target workflow,
- implementation steps,
- measurement method,
- failure mode notes.
