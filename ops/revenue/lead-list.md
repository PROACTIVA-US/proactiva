# Lead List

Use this as the working list for first outreach. Do not invent contacts. Fill only with leads you have verified from a public website, referral, or direct conversation.

## Starter Categories

- founder-led professional services firms
- operations-heavy agencies
- property management groups
- logistics and field service companies
- healthcare admin teams
- nonprofits with lean back offices
- local businesses with obvious intake or follow-up bottlenecks
- Law Firms with high volume of intake/onboarding
- Accounting/Legal Firms with high-volume intake
- High-volume HVAC or plumbing services
