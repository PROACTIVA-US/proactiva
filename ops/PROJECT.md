# Proactiva

## Mission

Proactiva is the revenue-facing company. Wildvine OS is the internal operating system that helps Proactiva find, diagnose, and resolve operational muddle.

The near-term offer is simple: identify avoidable cost, fix the workflow, verify the savings, and charge a percentage of the savings we can prove.

## What We Sell

- operational muddle diagnosis
- workflow cleanup and execution support
- verified cost-savings capture
- replication packs that let a similar company repeat the win
- founder-led delivery, then productized delivery

## Working Thesis

Most companies do not need more ideas. They need less confusion, fewer handoffs, clearer ownership, and a way to measure the money leaking out of the process.

Proactiva should be able to:

1. find a real operating bottleneck,
2. define the current cost of the muddle,
3. implement the fix,
4. prove the before/after result,
5. take a percentage of the savings.

## Revenue Model

- Discovery and diagnosis engagement
- Savings-share agreement tied to verified results
- Optional implementation fee for setup work
- Replication pack licensing once a pattern repeats
- Eventually: subscription access to a pack library

## Operating Shape

Wildvine OS is used internally to keep the work moving:

- research target accounts
- draft outreach
- run discovery calls
- estimate savings
- capture repeatable patterns
- turn patterns into replication packs

## Immediate Revenue Focus

- small and mid-sized organizations with visible operational friction
- founder-led or owner-led decision making
- workflows where delay, rework, or missed follow-up clearly costs money
- easy-to-measure improvements before deep technical integration
