# Savings-Share Template

Brief commercial terms for discussion only. Final terms should be reviewed by counsel.

## Simple Terms

- Parties: Proactiva and the client.
- Scope: one named process, team, or workflow.
- Goal: reduce avoidable cost, rework, delay, or waste.
- Baseline: the agreed starting point for measuring savings.
- Measurement period: the period used to compare before and after.
- Verified savings: the documented reduction in cost against the baseline, excluding unrelated changes.
- Fee: Proactiva receives `[__]%` of verified savings for the agreed period.
- Payment timing: client pays after the savings are measured and confirmed.
- Setup fee: optional fixed fee for discovery or implementation work.
- Term: starts on signature and runs until the agreed measurement period ends.
- Exclusions: changes caused by client layoffs, pricing changes, major market shifts, or unrelated restructuring are excluded unless agreed otherwise.
- Access: client provides the information reasonably needed to measure the process and savings.
- Confidentiality: both sides keep business information confidential.
- Termination: either side can end the arrangement with written notice, subject to payment for verified savings already earned.
- Dispute handling: the parties first review the numbers together and then escalate to a named decision-maker if needed.

## Plain-Language Summary

Proactiva helps a client reduce a specific operational problem. If the client can prove savings from the work, Proactiva gets a share of those savings.
