# Intelligence Brief: Henry Intelligent Machines (HIM)
## Source: meethenry.ai + Wissner-Gross announcement (April 7, 2026)

### What HIM Is
The first AI agent layer that assembles, operates, and scales **fleets of microbusinesses** for a single human owner. Not one business — a fleet. Diversified, automated, compounding.

Backed by 021T Capital (Alex Wissner-Gross). Announced April 7, 2026.

### How It Works
- Built on OpenClaw agent framework
- Lead agent "Henry" runs on Claude Opus 4.6
- Five-agent organization per user
- Agents handle execution; human supplies direction and taste
- Owner never leaves the loop

### What HIM Agents Do
**Publishing side:** Launch newsletters, produce research briefs, generate long-form guides, drive search traffic and ad revenue across agent-built sites.
**Services side:** White-label email marketing for local businesses, build apps/landing pages/websites at near-zero marginal cost.
**For tasks needing human hands:** Coordinate gig workers.

### The Thesis (from Wissner-Gross)
"AI is the first technology powerful enough to collapse coordination costs all the way to a single person." When coordination becomes nearly free, you don't need a new job — you need a fleet. HIM puts productive AI assets directly into individual hands.

### Relevance to Proactiva
HIM is general-purpose microbusiness automation. Proactiva is **domain-specific institutional compilation**. These are complementary, not competitive:

- **Partnership path:** Proactiva .vine packs become the specialized expertise HIM agents deploy. A HIM fleet operator who needs VA claims expertise deploys a Proactiva .vine. Revenue: licensing per .vine pack.
- **Own platform path:** Build our own OPC platform with Wildvine OS as the runtime. More sovereign. Slower. But every line of code is ours.
- **Hybrid:** Proactiva .vine packs work on BOTH platforms. Export format is portable.
